Seattle, WA, USA

Location

Seattle is located in the Puget Sound region of Washington state.

About

Weather

Seattle is known for its temperate climate. This is due to the fact that is situated between 2 large bodies of water.

Population

Approximately 750,000 people live within city limits, but the greater metropolitan area contains around 4 million.

Industry

Seattle (and its surrounding suburbs) are known as headquarters for many major companies such as Amazon, Starbucks, Boeing, and Microsoft.

Things To Do

  • Hiking
  • Seattle Symphony
  • Pacific Northwest Ballet
  • Pike Place Market
  • Space Needle
  • Seahawks, Sounders, and Mariners games
